Hey, everyone. This is my first proper post, and it’s about one of my favorite hobbies: photography — including cinematography and astrophotography. I discovered it in 2020, and my first photo was a simple sunset on the road. Since then, I’ve grown more and more drawn to it. Photography has fueled my creativity and pushed me to get outside far more often.

Enough talk — enjoy the shots.  
_Note: All of these were taken on a budget phone using an open-source camera app that lets me shoot RAW/DNG files._
