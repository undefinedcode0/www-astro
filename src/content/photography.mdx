Hey there, everybody. This going to be my first actual post, and it's going to be about one of my favorite hobbies, photography (includes cinematography, and astrophotography.) Photography has always been a hidden skill of mine since 2020, and my first photo included a sunset on the road. Ever since then, I've been more interested in this hobby of mine than ever. It has been a fuel for my early creativity and has helped me go outside more often. Enough yapping about this, please enjoy these photos of mine. Note: These photos were purely taken by my budget phone with a open-source camera app that lets me shoot Digital Negatives.
